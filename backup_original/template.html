With Villa Provin,

we have fulfilled our dream of a domicile on the Cote d`Azur in 2022, which we would very much like to share with you! The 280 sqm villa has three floors, various terraces and heated salt water pool. The property is located on a hillside plot with stepped terraces with olive and fruit trees and is sunny until late afternoon. A perfect holiday home in walking distance (15min) to the old town of Vence and just a 20min drive to the airport of Nice and the beach. With 10 beds in 5 bedrooms, the villa is ideal for up to 10 people and still offers space for retreat and privacy for each individual guest.

Enjoy the magnificent view over Vence, down to the Mediterranean Sea in a modern and sophisticated ambience. Relax in the sun on the various terraces, go for walks and bike rides directly from the villa, enjoy the nearby culture (art exhibitions, museums) or do your sporty exercises on the Peloton Bike+ with your own fitness coach. Enjoy different restaurants or the local market in Vence and feel the South of France culture. The latest technology (TV, Sonos sound system), a heated salt water pool with a fantastic outdoor area with its own boules court and a unique barbecue area will give you and your family and friends the perfect holiday time.
