  • Master I



    The east facing master bedroom I, has a bathroom en suite equipped with a shower and a toilet and is located on the ground floor of the villa. It offers a luxurious 180x200cm Hästens box spring bed and a direct access to the pool via a private terrace. Master bedroom I is equipped with its own air conditioning system.

  • Master II



    The master bedroom II, the largest bedroom with a 180x200 four-poster bed, is located in the basement with en-suite bathroom equipped with shower and toilet. The Master II has a covered terrace with a view to the south over olive trees, surrounding neighborhood, Vence and the Mediterranean Sea. The terrace leads to the barbecue-, boule- and pool area. Master II is equipped with its own air conditioning.

  • Bedroom I



    Bedroom I is located on the first floor. It is the smallest bedroom in the villa but cosy. It is equipped with two 90x200cm beds which can be placed together or separately. Please specify when booking, if you want single or double bed configuration for this room. The view from the window is west towards Vence. The bedroom I shares a bathroom and separate toilet with bedroom II and III and is equipped with its own air conditioning.

  • Bedroom II



    Bedroom II is located on the first floor. It is equipped with a 180x200cm bed and offers a remarkable view to the west over the old town of Vence, especially by night. Bedroom II shares a bathroom and separate toilet with bedrooms I and III and is equipped with its own air conditioning.

  • Bedroom III



    Bedroom III is located on the first floor. It is equipped with two 90x200cm beds which can be placed together or separately. Please specify when booking, if you want single or double bed configuration for this room. Bedroom III faces south and offers a remarkable view to the surrounding countryside and the Mediterranean Sea, especially when lying in bed and waking up in the morning. Bedroom III shares a bathroom and separate toilet with bedrooms I and II and is equipped with its own air conditioning.

  • TV I



    In the basement is a common TV room, with a Peloton Bike+ with shoes size 36, 39, 42, dumbbells and 4 yoga mats. The room is equipped with air conditioning.

  • TV II



    On the first floor is a large common room with TV, recliner and futon bed that serves as a sofa or guest bed for additional 1-2 people (futon bed pulls out 80x200 or 160x200). The room has a low ceiling height on each side and has a window to the east overlooking the pool and a skylight and is the perfect play and relax room for kids.

  • Living room and kitchen



    From the living room and the kitchen you can reach the south-facing terrace with panoramic views of the hills of Nice, Vence and Cap Antibes and the Mediterranean Sea. The terrace is equipped with 2 awnings and dining area and lounge chairs. From the kitchen you have access to the guest toilet and the small utility room with washing machine, dryer, ironing board and iron, vacuum cleaner.

  • Amazing Barbecue Area



    The Villa is equipped with an OFYR fire plate, grill, fire bowl, outdoor cooking appliance. A real wood fire blazes in the middle. The concept is unique. Barbecue in a circle! The heavy steel gets hot differently. More on the inside, less on the outside! With a little practice, you'll have fun playing with temperatures and food. Everything that can be barbecued can be placed on the OFYR. Barbecuing becomes part of your holiday and increases your quality of life in relaxed hours as a social event.

  • Heated salt water pool and boules court



    The pool area is fenced and thus secured according to the latest standards, which is particularly convenient when traveling with small children. The pool is surrounded by a long, sunny wooden terrace with sun loungers, outdoor shower (hot/cold) and a covered pool house with seating. Below the pool is a private boules court next to the barbecue area. The salt water pool can be heated on request and is about 4,5x10m.

About Vence

Vence is a French commune with 19,315 inhabitants (as of January 1, 2020) in the Alpes-Maritimes department in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region. Vence is located in the arrondissement of Grasse. The commune belongs to the Métropole Nice Côte d'Azur, covers an area of 39.23 square kilometers and parts of the commune are located in the Regional Natural Park Préalpes d'Azur. Vence is one of the famous tourist destinations in Provence. It is perched high on a cliff, can be reached quickly from the coast and has the character of a barely touched pre-Alpine town. The inhabitants of Vence call themselves Vençois. For more information...
