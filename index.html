The Village

Discover Vence

Perched high above the Côte d'Azur, Vence is one of the most enchanting medieval hill towns in all of France. Wander the cobbled alleyways of the old town, discover the legendary Matisse Chapel just minutes from the villa, and lose yourself in the scent of lavender and orange blossom at the weekly market.

World-class restaurants, authentic Provençal bistros, galleries, and boutiques — Vence offers the perfect blend of tranquillity and culture. And Nice, Cannes, Monaco, and the beaches of the Riviera are all within easy reach.
